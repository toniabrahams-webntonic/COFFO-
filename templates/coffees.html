TYPES OF COFFEE

We offer a wide variety of coffee types from different regions around the world. Each type has its unique flavor profile and characteristics.

  • Arabica - Smooth and aromatic
  • Robusta - Strong and full-bodied
  • Liberica - Unique and fruity
  • Excelsa - Tart and fruity
BEAN VARIETIES

Our selection of premium coffee beans comes from the best growing regions worldwide. We carefully source and roast each variety to perfection.

  • Ethiopian Yirgacheffe - Floral and citrus notes
  • Colombian Supremo - Balanced and nutty
  • Sumatra Mandheling - Earthy and full-bodied
  • Guatemalan Antigua - Chocolate and spice notes
COFFEE & PASTRY

  • Freshly baked croissants

  • Gluten-free and vegan options

  • Mobile ordering available
COLD BREW

Experience the smooth and refreshing taste of our cold brew coffee, perfect for hot summer days or whenever you need a cool pick-me-up.

  • Slow-steeped for 12 hours
  • Served over ice
  • Available in various flavors
  • Dairy and non-dairy options
ESPRESSO

Our espresso shots are crafted to deliver a strong and flavorful experience, perfect for true coffee lovers who appreciate the art of espresso making.

  • Rich and intense flavor
  • Made with freshly ground beans
  • Served in traditional demitasse cups
  • Available as single or double shots
CAPPUCCINO

Enjoy a perfectly balanced cappuccino with rich foam and bold espresso, crafted to provide a delightful coffee experience.

  • Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and foam
  • Topped with a sprinkle of cocoa or cinnamon
  • Served in a wide-brimmed cup
  • Customizable with flavored syrups
